Terms and Conditions

Here are the terms and conditions for adopting an animal:

  • 🐾 1) The Adopter warrants that the information he provided is accurate to the best of his knowledge. In particular, the Adopter warrants that he is over 21 years in age.
  • 🐾 2) The Adopter agrees to provide HKSCDA with the Adopter’s Hong Kong ID card for verification.
  • 🐾 3) The Adopter agrees to provide the Adopted Animal with a proper home and the best care the Adopter can provide.
  • 🐾 4) The Adopter agrees that the Adopted Animal must be neutered.
  • 🐾 5) The Adopter agrees, upon receiving written notice from HKSCDA, to allow representatives of HKSCDA to conduct visits or calls.
  • 🐾 6) The Adopter agrees to inform HKSCDA immediately if the Adopter decides to abandon the Adopted Animal.
  • 🐾 7) The Adopter agrees not to transfer the Adopted Animal to any third party without prior written consent from HKSCDA.
  • 🐾 8) The Adopter agrees not to threaten or abuse the Adopted Animal.
  • 🐾 9) The Adopter agrees to send photos of the Adopted Animal to HKSCDA for promoting adoption.
  • 🐾 10) The Adopter agrees to contact HKSCDA if the Adopted Animal develops any health conditions within seven days after adoption.
  • 🐾 11) The Adopter agrees, if the application is approved, to pay the non-refundable adoption fees.
  • 🐾 12) HKSCDA may, at its sole discretion, request the Adopter to return the Adopted Animal to HKSCDA.
  • 🐾 13) The Adopter agrees to accept full responsibility for any risks associated with adopting the Adoption Animal.
  • 🐾 14) In case of disputes, all decisions and interpretation of the provisions in this terms and conditions by HKSCDA are final.
  • 🐾 15) The Chinese translation of this terms and conditions is for reference only.
