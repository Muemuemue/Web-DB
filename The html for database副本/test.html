About Us

We are a group of cat lovers dedicated to rescuing and providing care for stray cats, offering them warmth and a new home. We believe that every stray cat deserves love and protection.

Our Mission Includes:

Rescue and Placement: Through street patrols, rescue operations, and support from volunteers, we provide essential medical care for stray cats and work tirelessly to find them loving forever homes.

Awareness and Education: By organizing community events and promoting the importance of spaying and neutering, we aim to reduce the number of stray cats and advocate for harmonious coexistence with animals.

Resource Connection: We collaborate with various charitable organizations and individual donors to provide food, medical care, and temporary shelters for stray cats.
